About Me

I’m a project and operations lead who enjoys turning messy, high-stakes workflows into calm, predictable systems. Over the last years at Amazon Prime Video I’ve been the person in the middle of troubleshooting, QA and partner support, connecting global teams and making sure complex issues actually get resolved – and stay resolved.

I’m most at home when I can blend structured thinking + data + people: designing audit frameworks, rebuilding error taxonomies, redefining how tickets flow between teams, and coaching SMEs to align around one way of working. Recent work includes implementing a global Audit the Auditor program, standardizing multi-site QA systems, and integrating partner-facing workflows without breaking SLAs.

If you’re looking for someone who can own an operational problem end-to-end – from mapping the chaos to building the process, training the team and tracking the impact – that’s the space where I add the most value.
