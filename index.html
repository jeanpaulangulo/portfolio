About Me

Project Manager and Operations Lead with 10+ years of experience in streaming, digital media and global operations (Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, Paramount+, DAZN, Fox Networks, Antena 3). I specialize in designing and scaling workflows, reducing operational friction, and turning complex, multi-team problems into measurable, sustainable solutions.

Across my career, I've led initiatives that reduced duplicated troubleshooting by 30%, cut time-to-resolution for complex cases by 15%, enabled 24/7 coverage with existing headcount, and improved delivery reliability for partners like FIBA, Paramount+ and YouTube Studios. My sweet spot sits between operations, product, and technology: aligning teams, standardizing processes, and using data and tools (Jira, Slack workflows, automation, analytics) to drive better decisions and customer outcomes.
